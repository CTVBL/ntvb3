GYMNASIUMS

Listed below are the gyms available this season. Our permits require that no food is taken into the gymnasiums, proper footwear is worn at all times, and gyms are left in the same condition as when we enter. Thanks!!!
 

LAWRENCE PARK CI - 125 Chatsworth Drive, Toronto, ON



Directions:
Located on the south side of Lawrence, just east of Avenue.  It's on Chatsworth, but you can't turn onto this street as it's a one way.

Take Greer Rd south from Lawrence then turn right on Chudleigh. Turn right onto Chatsworth and the school is on your left.

There is ample street and school parking available.

Enter the school at the main doors off of Chatsworth.


SUNNYVIEW JR & SR PS - 450 Blythwood Road, Toronto, ON




Directions:
Located on the north side of Blythwood between Mount Pleasant and Bayview. 

Ample parking is available at the rear of the school. 

Enter the school at the rear near the parking lot.


BLYTHWOOD JPS - 2 Strathgowan Crescent, Toronto, ON




Directions:
Located on the north side of Blythwood between Mount Pleasant and Bayview. 

It is located at the corner of Strathgowan Crescent and Blythwood, just west of Sunnyview Public School.

Street parking is available.


BENNINGTON HEIGHTS ES - 76 Bennington Heights Dr, Toronto, ON




Directions:
Follow Bayview Ave south and continue straight on Bayview Heights Dr. Turn east at Bennington Heights Dr.


LEASIDE HIGHSCHOOL - 200 Hanna Road, Toronto, ON




Directions:
Located east of Bayview off of Eglinton. Take Eglinton east and turn right on Hanna Road. The school is on the right.

We are using the second and smaller of the two gyms.  Upon entering the school go downstairs to the second, smaller gym.

Ample school parking.
