LEAGUE REGISTRATION

LEAGUE INFO

  • The season runs from October 3, 2017 to May 15, 2018
  • Games are held at local public schools around Bayview & Eglinton
  • Level of play is Recreational to Competitive
  • 4 players on the court with a minimum of 1 female and 1 male playing at all times
    (We suggest a roster size of 5-6 people)
  • Games are played Tuesdays evenings from 6:45pm to 9:45pm
  • A ladder system is used for scheduling ensuring teams play at the appropriate skill level

REGISTRATION OPEN

  • Registration opened to new teams on September 8, 2017
  • Registration fees for new teams is $1650 per team
  • Returning teams must pay a $100 deposit to guarantee their spot for the season
  • If registering a team, the team captain is responsible to pay fees for their entire team.
    i.e. submit 1 cheque or Interac email transfer, not several
  • To register a team, please complete the REGISTRATION FORM (right click, save target as) and email to contact@centraltorontovolleyball.com.

WAIVER FORMS

  • Each player in the league must sign a WAIVER FORM (right click, save target as). 
  • Captain's are responsible for collecting waiver forms for their team. Waiver forms should be submitted with your payment. Temporary players and subs must also sign a waiver. Please ensure each player that plays on your team signs one.
  • For information on the insurance please review INSURANCE COVERAGE..
